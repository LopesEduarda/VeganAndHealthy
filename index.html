About Us

Vegetarian cuisine is a term that refers to foods that are prepared according to vegetarian standards, and therefore do not include meat or any other products made from animal tissue.


More than a diet without animal products, Vegan and Healtry proposes a minimum impact mentality for the environment and for all living beings. It is the idea of a gastronomy that lives in balance with the ecosystem.
